Mug Brownie

A simple recipe for when you want something sweet and cannot wait long. It is ready in a few minutes using ingredients that are easy to find at home.

Prep time: 2 minutes Difficulty: very easy Made in the microwave

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons wheat flour
  • 3 tablespoons white or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate powder or cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter, coconut oil, or sunflower oil
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Chocolate chips or one small chocolate square, if you want

Step by step

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, chocolate powder, and salt in a microwave-safe mug.
  2. Add the melted butter and milk. Stir well with a fork until the batter is smooth.
  3. Add chocolate chips or press one small chocolate square into the center of the batter.
  4. Microwave on high power for about 1 minute.

Tip

To keep the brownie creamier, be careful not to microwave it for too long. If needed, heat it for a few more seconds until it reaches the texture you like.
